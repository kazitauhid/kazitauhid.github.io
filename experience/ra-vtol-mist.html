01 · Contributions

What I Did Here

My responsibilities spanned three tracks — hands-on engineering work on the VTOL UAV programme, teaching and mentorship of undergraduate students, and administrative work for academic accreditation.

✈️
Fixed-Wing VTOL Airframe Design
Contributed to airframe design across four prototype generations — wing geometry, fuselage layout, transition-mode aerodynamics, and structural integration. Used SolidWorks for CAD modelling and XFLR5 for aerodynamic analysis.
🔧
Piston-Engine Propulsion Integration
Worked on the integration of a piston-engine propulsion system — a non-standard choice for VTOL UAVs requiring careful management of vibration, fuel system routing, weight distribution, and engine-mount design.
🛫
Prototype Build & Flight Testing
Hands-on fabrication, assembly, and flight testing of all four prototype generations. Collected flight data and fed lessons learned into the next design iteration. See the VTOL project case study → for prototype-by-prototype photos and detail.
📐
Hill-Terrain Mission Profile Engineering
The platform was designed specifically for surveillance over hill terrain, requiring careful trade-offs in endurance, payload, and operational ceiling. Contributed to the mission-profile analysis and operational requirements definition.
🎓
Engineering Sessional Class Instruction
Delivered laboratory instructions to undergraduate Aeronautical Engineering students in Engineering Sessional Classes — covering hands-on work in design, simulation, and aircraft-systems experiments.
🧪
Assisted — Two Undergraduate Thesis Projects
Assisted two undergraduate teams working on drone research projects, providing guidance on system design, hardware integration, and academic writing:

· Real-Time Inter-Drone Collision Mitigation for UAV Swarm Architecture — extending the swarm safety envelope from my own thesis work.
· Sensor Data Fusion and Human Tracking in Swarm-Based Surveillance — expanding the perception stack of the swarm platform.
🤝
Knowledge Transfer — Drone Research & Tooling
Onboarded junior researchers on drone design and development — transferring knowledge of aeronautical design tools, UAV build procedures, avionics integration, and field-test protocols across both fixed-wing and multi-rotor platforms.
📜
Washington Accord Accreditation Documentation
Contributed to the preparation of administrative documents required for the Washington Accord accreditation of the MIST Aeronautical Engineering programme. The Washington Accord is an international agreement among engineering education accreditation bodies that recognises the substantial equivalence of engineering degrees.
🏛
BAETE Submission Preparation
Prepared submission documents for the Board of Accreditation for Engineering and Technical Education (BAETE) — the national accreditation body responsible for evaluating engineering programmes in Bangladesh.
📊
Programme Outcomes & Curriculum Mapping
Assisted in mapping programme learning outcomes against accreditation criteria, preparing curriculum documentation, and compiling supporting evidence required by the accreditation review process.

03 · Outcomes

What This Role Delivered

By the end of the appointment, the programme had successfully delivered four progressive prototype generations, with the final platform validating the piston-engine VTOL approach for hill-terrain surveillance missions.

On the academic side, two co-supervised undergraduate theses were completed — both extending my own thesis work on swarm UAV systems — and the Aeronautical Engineering programme advanced its accreditation submission to BAETE.

This experience consolidated my hands-on aircraft design and prototyping skills while exposing me to the academic-administration side of running a research-active engineering department — an unusually broad scope for a six-month role.
